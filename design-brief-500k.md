# Yellow Lotus Consulting Group website redesign brief

Goal: turn yellowlotuscg.com into a $500k/year-worthy consulting website.

Business positioning:
- Yellow Lotus Consulting Group helps small businesses and operators install practical AI, CRM/speed-to-lead systems, internal dashboards, automation, cloud/DevOps, and GRC/security workflows.
- The site must sell trust, money outcomes, operational clarity, and technical competence.
- Avoid vague AI hype. Avoid generic lotus/cyber art repeated everywhere.
- Preserve approval gates and privacy-first/local-first positioning.

Primary conversion:
- Book/request an AI workflow assessment.
- Secondary: review service lanes and proof/case-study style examples.

Tone:
- Premium, calm, practical, operator-friendly.
- Feels like Stripe/Linear/Apple quality, but with Yellow Lotus identity.
- No em dashes.

Design direction:
- More white/cream editorial space mixed with deep premium dark sections.
- Use gold/jade sparingly as accents, not every card.
- Replace repetitive symbolic panels with practical business operating visuals.
- Show workflows: lead intake, CRM, follow-up, dashboards, automation, secure cloud, AI assistant workers.
- Build sections around outcomes and offers, not generic capabilities.

Suggested structure:
1. Hero: "AI systems that turn scattered work into booked revenue, cleaner operations, and faster decisions."
2. Trust strip: AI workflow assessment, CRM/speed-to-lead, dashboards, GRC/cloud/DevOps.
3. Offer ladder: assessment, roadmap, build, retainer.
4. Money operating system: how Alfred/Yellow Lotus finds bottlenecks, builds automations, measures outcomes.
5. Proof cards: Pro Tracker, Landscape Profit Scheduler, Alfred dashboard, CloudRiskIQ/security.
6. Process: Diagnose, design, build, train, measure.
7. CTA: request an assessment.

Constraints:
- Single static index.html, no dependencies/build step.
- Must remain deployable as-is to Vercel or static host.
- Use local assets in assets/.
- Responsive, accessible, fast.
- Do not deploy publicly until owner approval.
