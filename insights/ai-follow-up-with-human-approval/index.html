AI follow-up without losing human approval

AI can help with customer follow-up, but the safest workflow keeps people in control of customer-facing messages.

AI is strongest as a drafting layer

AI can turn notes into a clean reply, summarize a call, suggest next steps, and prepare follow-up language. That saves time without handing over judgment.

Approval gates protect the business

A customer-facing message can affect trust, pricing, scope, or legal exposure. Approval gates make sure sensitive steps stay under human review.

The best workflow is visible

The owner should be able to see drafts, reminders, stale leads, and sent messages. A secure dashboard makes AI support easier to trust because the process is not hidden.
