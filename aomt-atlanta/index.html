About

Authentic Muay Thai training in West Midtown Atlanta.

AOMT is built on authentic Muay Thai and the culture that surrounds it: discipline, respect, repetition, and steady progress.

Whether you are stepping into the gym for the first time or adding striking to an existing routine, the training environment is focused, practical, and welcoming.
